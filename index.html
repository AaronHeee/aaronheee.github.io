Hey folks! I am a third year Ph.D. student at CSE, UC San Diego, advised by Prof. Julian McAuley. My research interests are recommender systems and broader personalized machine learning, such as personalized image retrieval and personalized natural language generation tasks.
I strongly believe that good recommender systems should be nice friends instead of feeding machines. To this end, my research aims to make recommender systems become more (1) accurate, (2) explainable and (3) interactive.
